REEKADO BANKS

Project Manager - "Daddy To Many" Album

Project Overview

As Project Manager for REEKADO BANKS' "Daddy To Many" album, I managed the complete album production and release cycle over a nine-month period. This role involved coordinating creative teams, maintaining production timelines, and supporting the comprehensive release execution for an established Afrobeats artist.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Album Timeline Management: Developed and maintained comprehensive production schedule from recording through release
  • Creative Team Coordination: Managed relationships and schedules for producers, engineers, featured artists, and creative directors
  • Release Execution Support: Coordinated all aspects of album rollout including distribution, marketing, and promotional activities
  • Studio Session Management: Scheduled and coordinated recording sessions, ensuring efficient use of studio time
  • Budget Tracking: Monitored project expenses and maintained financial accountability
  • Quality Control: Ensured deliverables met artist and label standards throughout production
  • Stakeholder Communication: Served as liaison between artist, label, management, and external partners
  • Release Campaign Coordination: Supported marketing and promotional campaign execution

About REEKADO BANKS

REEKADO BANKS is a multi-award-winning Nigerian singer and songwriter known for his distinctive voice and hit singles. With a career spanning multiple successful albums and collaborations with top African and international artists, he is one of the most respected voices in contemporary Afrobeats.

Managing an album project for an established artist requires balancing creative vision with commercial objectives while coordinating complex production and release schedules.
