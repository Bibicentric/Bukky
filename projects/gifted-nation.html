Project Overview

As Tour, Operations, and Project Manager for GIFTED NATION RECORD LABEL, I managed comprehensive touring operations, team coordination, logistics, and project execution for label artists OHLA and TARIE. This role involved multi-faceted responsibilities supporting emerging artists' career development over a 15-month period.

  • Touring Operations: Managed all aspects of tour planning, logistics, and execution for label artists
  • Team Coordination: Led coordination between artists, crew, management, and external partners

  • Vendor Relations: Built and maintained relationships with venues, promoters, and service providers
