Project Manager - "No Cap" Podcast

Project Overview

As Project Manager for DOTUN's "No Cap" podcast, I managed comprehensive production timelines, talent coordination, and release planning for digital podcast content. This role involved coordinating all aspects of podcast production from recording through distribution and promotion.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Production Timelines: Developed and maintained production schedules for regular podcast episode releases
  • Talent Coordination: Managed booking, scheduling, and logistics for podcast guests and featured talent
  • Release Planning: Coordinated content release schedule across podcast platforms and promotional channels
  • Recording Coordination: Scheduled and managed podcast recording sessions ensuring technical and creative quality
  • Post-Production Management: Oversaw editing, audio production, and content preparation for release
  • Distribution Coordination: Managed podcast distribution across multiple streaming platforms
  • Promotional Support: Coordinated promotional activities including social media, clips, and marketing materials
  • Team Management: Coordinated with producers, editors, and creative team members

About DOTUN (DO2DTUN)

DOTUN, also known as DO2DTUN, is a prominent Nigerian media personality and on-air personality. The "No Cap" podcast represents his venture into long-form digital content, featuring candid conversations with entertainment industry figures and cultural influencers.

Managing podcast production requires coordinating multiple creative and technical elements while maintaining consistent content quality and release schedules.
