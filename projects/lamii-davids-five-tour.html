LAMII - David's Five Tour

Tour Management

Project Overview

The David's Five Tour was a multi-city concert series featuring rising Afrobeats artist LAMII. As Tour Manager, I orchestrated all logistical, operational, and coordination aspects of the tour across multiple cities on the East Coast.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Multi-City Tour Logistics: Planned and executed comprehensive logistics for concerts across Philadelphia and Maryland/DC metro areas
  • Travel Coordination: Managed all travel arrangements including ground transportation, accommodations, and inter-city transfers
  • Daily Operations: Oversaw day-to-day tour activities, maintaining schedules and ensuring artist needs were met
  • Venue Coordination: Liaised with venue management, production teams, and local promoters at each tour stop
  • Budget Management: Tracked tour expenses and managed financial operations on the road
  • Artist Support: Ensured LAMII's comfort, safety, and readiness for each performance
