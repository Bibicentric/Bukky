OXLADE Tour

Tour Management

Project Overview

Serving as Tour Manager for internationally acclaimed Afrobeats artist OXLADE, I orchestrated complex tour logistics, facilitated communication between multiple stakeholders, and ensured seamless execution across all tour dates.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Tour Planning: Developed comprehensive tour strategies including routing, scheduling, and resource allocation
  • Scheduling: Created and maintained detailed itineraries coordinating performances, travel, promo activities, and rest periods
  • Logistics Management: Oversaw all operational aspects including transportation, accommodations, equipment, and crew coordination
  • Stakeholder Communication: Served as primary liaison between artist management, venues, promoters, and production teams
  • Contract Coordination: Ensured all contractual obligations were met at each venue
  • Crisis Management: Addressed real-time challenges and implemented solutions to keep the tour on track

About OXLADE

OXLADE is a Nigerian singer and songwriter who gained international recognition with his breakout single "Away" and collaborations with global artists. His unique voice and emotional delivery have made him one of the most sought-after artists in the Afrobeats genre.

Managing tours for an artist of OXLADE's caliber requires meticulous attention to detail, strong vendor relationships, and the ability to adapt to changing circumstances while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism.

Key Achievements

  • Coordinated complex international tour logistics for a high-profile artist
  • Maintained effective communication channels across multiple time zones and stakeholders
  • Ensured on-time arrivals and performance readiness at every tour stop
  • Built and maintained strong relationships with venue partners and production teams
  • Implemented efficient systems for tour documentation and financial tracking
