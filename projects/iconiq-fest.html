ICONIQ FEST

Live Event Management

Project Overview

ICONIQ FEST was a major multi-artist live music event held in December 2025. As the Live Event Manager, I was responsible for the complete onsite execution, ensuring seamless coordination across all aspects of the festival.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Onsite Execution: Oversaw all day-of event operations, ensuring smooth execution from doors open to final curtain
  • Artist Coordination: Managed logistics and requirements for headlining artists Chike, BNXN, and Shallipopi
  • Stage Schedules: Developed and maintained precise timing schedules for performances, transitions, and technical setup
  • Backstage Operations: Coordinated green room management, artist hospitality, and backstage flow
  • Team Leadership: Led cross-functional teams including production, security, and hospitality staff
  • Crisis Management: Handled real-time problem-solving and ensured event continuity

Featured Artists

Chike

Nigerian singer, songwriter, and actor known for his soulful voice and Afro-soul music

BNXN (fka Buju)

Rising Afrobeats star with hit singles and collaborations across the African music scene

Shallipopi

Fast-rising Nigerian artist known for his unique sound and energetic performances

Key Achievements

  • Successfully coordinated a multi-artist event with thousands of attendees
  • Maintained strict adherence to performance schedules with zero delays
  • Ensured artist satisfaction through meticulous attention to technical and hospitality requirements
  • Facilitated smooth transitions between performances and technical setups
  • Created a professional and efficient backstage environment
