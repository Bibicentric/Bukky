About Rolling Stone Africa

Rolling Stone Africa is the African edition of the legendary Rolling Stone magazine, covering music, culture, and entertainment across the continent. As part of MWANKOM GROUP's portfolio, the publication has become a leading voice in African music journalism and cultural commentary.

Working at this level required understanding the intersection of media, music business, and African cultural movements while maintaining the high editorial standards of a globally recognized brand.
