Project Overview

As Project Manager for ENGAGE MUSIC LABEL in partnership with MAD SOLUTIONS DISTRIBUTION CO, I supported digital content planning, project coordination, and label operations. This role involved managing various label projects while supporting the broader operational needs of a music distribution company.

  • Timeline Management: Maintained project schedules and ensured timely delivery of releases and campaigns

  • Release Planning: Coordinated release strategies and promotional rollouts for label content
  • Administrative Support: Managed project documentation, tracking, and reporting
