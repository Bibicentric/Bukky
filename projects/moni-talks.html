Project Manager - Entertainment Talk Show

Project Overview

As Project Manager for MONI TALKS, a televised entertainment talk show, I coordinated comprehensive production logistics, scheduling, and execution. This role required managing the complex moving parts of television production while maintaining quality and meeting broadcast deadlines.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Production Logistics: Coordinated all production elements including studio bookings, equipment, crew scheduling, and technical requirements
  • Scheduling: Managed complex production calendars coordinating guest availability, crew schedules, and broadcast deadlines
  • Execution Management: Oversaw show production from pre-production through post-production and broadcast
  • Guest Coordination: Managed booking and logistics for entertainment industry guests and talent
  • Team Management: Coordinated production crew, technical staff, and creative team members
  • Budget Oversight: Tracked production expenses and maintained financial accountability
  • Quality Control: Ensured production met broadcast standards and creative vision
  • Problem Solving: Addressed real-time production challenges and scheduling conflicts
