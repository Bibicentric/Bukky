Project Manager - "King of the Wolves" Album

Project Overview

As Project Manager for ALPHA P's "King of the Wolves" album, I coordinated comprehensive album production timelines, managed studio sessions, and ensured delivery of all creative deliverables. This eight-month project involved supporting an emerging artist's debut major project through all phases of production.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Album Production Timelines: Developed and maintained detailed production schedule from pre-production through final delivery
  • Studio Session Coordination: Scheduled and managed recording sessions, ensuring efficient studio time usage and creative productivity
  • Creative Deliverables: Tracked and coordinated all album components including recordings, mixing, mastering, and artwork
  • Team Coordination: Managed relationships with producers, engineers, featured artists, and creative team members
  • Budget Tracking: Monitored project expenses and maintained financial accountability throughout production
  • Quality Assurance: Ensured all deliverables met artistic vision and technical standards
  • Timeline Management: Maintained project momentum and addressed scheduling challenges
  • Communication: Served as central point of contact for all project stakeholders
