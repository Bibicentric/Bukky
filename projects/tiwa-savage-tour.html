TIWA SAVAGE

Tour & Project Manager - North American Tour(s)

Project Overview

As Tour and Project Manager for internationally acclaimed Afrobeats superstar TIWA SAVAGE, I orchestrated multiple North American tour dates, managing all aspects of international touring logistics, artist support, and production coordination. This role required managing complex cross-border operations, high-profile venue relationships, and maintaining the highest standards of professionalism for one of Africa's biggest music exports.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Tour Logistics: Planned and executed comprehensive logistics for multiple tour dates across North America, coordinating travel, accommodations, and ground transportation
  • Travel Coordination: Managed international and domestic travel arrangements, visa processing, customs documentation, and itinerary planning for artist and crew
  • Budget Management: Created and maintained tour budgets, tracked expenses, negotiated rates, and ensured financial accountability across all tour operations
  • Venue Liaison: Served as primary contact with venue management, promoters, and production teams at each tour stop
  • Release Support: Coordinated project management support for music releases concurrent with tour activities
  • Advance Planning: Conducted venue advances, technical riders review, and pre-show coordination
  • Day-of-Show Management: Oversaw all show-day operations including load-in, soundcheck, performance, and load-out
  • Artist Support: Ensured artist comfort, safety, and readiness throughout the tour

About TIWA SAVAGE

TIWA SAVAGE is one of Africa's biggest music stars and a global ambassador for Afrobeats music. With multiple awards, international collaborations, and chart-topping hits, she has performed on stages worldwide and broken barriers for African artists in the global music industry.

Managing tours for an artist of TIWA SAVAGE's stature requires exceptional organizational skills, industry relationships, and the ability to maintain the highest professional standards while managing the complexities of international touring.
