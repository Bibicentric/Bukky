AFRO PLUS FESTIVAL

Live Event Management

Project Overview

AFRO PLUS FESTIVAL showcased the best of Afrobeats and African music culture in August 2025. I managed the complete event lifecycle from pre-production through execution, ensuring a world-class experience for both artists and attendees.

Role & Responsibilities

  • End-to-End Event Logistics: Coordinated all aspects of event planning, production, and execution
  • Rehearsals: Organized and managed technical rehearsals, soundchecks, and run-throughs for all artists
  • Call Times: Established and communicated precise schedules for all stakeholders, ensuring punctuality
  • Real-Time Show Execution: Managed live event flow, making critical decisions to maintain schedule and quality
  • Artist Coordination: Facilitated seamless experiences for Wande Coal, Lamii, and Sawea
  • Vendor Management: Coordinated with production companies, catering, security, and technical teams

Featured Artists

Wande Coal

Legendary Nigerian singer and songwriter, pioneer of Afro-soul and R&B fusion
